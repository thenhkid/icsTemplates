Simple Self Monitoring

Monitoring hypertension is convenient with “My BP Coach”, an I.C. Sciences m|d-Health Smart Care App. Simply Connect to any supported monitoring device with Bluetooth using an iPhone or Android device and begin tracking health statistics. All health data is backed up securely to the cloud and can be accessed remotely from a phone, tablet or computer. This allows for more time to enjoy life without worrying about the logistics of managing a disease.

Remote Patient Care
